Why INVEST in AGRICULTURE?

Explore the many reasons why AGRICULTURE is an attractive investment.

RESILIENT

More stable than the STOCK MARKET. It is the ONLY major economic sector that defied the economic downturn, posting positive growth while the over-all economy went on a steep dive.

HIGH DEMAND FOR FOOD

By 2050, the WORLD POPULATION will grow to over 10 BN people. Furthermore, 3 BN people will enter the middle-class causing FOOD DEMAND to skyrocket.

HEDGE AGAINST INFLATION

Proven tool to protect your investment portfolio against inflation. It produces returns in both the form of CAPITAL GROWTH AND INCOME GROWTH. It is a good hedge against inflation because food prices tend to rise with inflation, providing a natural protection against the eroding value of currency.

SOCIAL IMPACT

AGRIBUSINESS has a massive SOCIAL and ENVIRONMENTAL FOOTPRINT. It represents 10% of GLOBAL CONSUMER SPENDING, 40% of EMPLOYMENT, and 30% of GREEN-HOUSE GAS EMISSION. Also, it provides livelihoods for millions of people worldwide, particularly in rural areas. It is a major source of employment, income, and food security for many communities, contributing to poverty reduction and economic development.
